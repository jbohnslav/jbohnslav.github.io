About

December 10, 2024 · Jim Robinson-Bohnslav

I’m a machine learning engineer at Zoox, training and shipping foundation models for robotic perception.

Previously, I worked at Cobot as the first perception hire and second SWE. I did zero-to-one prototyping, evaluated sensor suites, designed the perception system, and implemented initial prototypes.

Prior to that, I was a research scientist at Perceptive Automata, training models to help AVs understand human behavior.

In my academic life, I did my PhD in Neuroscience at Harvard, building novel computer vision pipelines for analyzing animal behavior in Chris Harvey’s lab.
